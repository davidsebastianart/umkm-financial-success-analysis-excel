## üìå Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **interactive Excel dashboard** that analyzes the key success factors of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (UMKM/MSMEs) in Indonesia.

The analysis focuses on **financial health**, **marketing efforts**, **digital adoption**, and **professional consultation** to identify actionable insights that can help improve UMKM success rates.

## üóÇÔ∏è Dataset

- **Source**: [Kaggle - MSME Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/eskobar/msme-dataset)
- **Records**: 251 UMKM profiles
- **Target Variable**: `Success_Label` (1 = Successful, 0 = Not Successful)
- **Key Attributes**: Age, Education, Initial Capital, Financial Record Keeping, Marketing Effort, Internet Usage, Professional Advice, Industry Experience, Gender, etc.

**Derived Features**:
- `Financial_Score` & `Financial_Score_Category` (Strong / Moderate / Weak)
- `Marketing_Score_Category` (High / Medium / Low)
- Readable label columns for visualization

## üîç Key Insights

- **Overall UMKM Success Rate**: **24.80%** Successful
- **Best Performing Group**: UMKM with **Moderate Financial Score** have the **highest success rate (53.60%)**
- **Marketing Impact**: High marketing effort category achieves **42.40%** success rate (significantly higher than Medium & Low)
- **Gender**: Male-owned businesses show **56%** success rate vs **44%** for female-owned
- **Moderate financial discipline** outperforms both "Strong" and "Weak" categories in this dataset

## Recommendations to Improve UMKM Success Rate

1. **Prioritize Financial Management Improvements**  
   - UMKM with "Strong" Financial Score Category show a success rate of **24.40%**, while "Weak" only reach **24.00%** (minimal gap visible in this dataset).  
   - However, the **"Moderate"** category performs best at **53.60%** success rate.  
   - **Recommendation**: Focus training on financial record-keeping and initial capital access to shift more UMKM from "Weak" to "Moderate/Strong".

2. **Strengthen Marketing Effort & Digital Adoption**  
   - UMKM with "High" Marketing Effort Category achieve the highest success rate at **42.40%**, compared to **28.40%** (Medium) and **29.20%** (Low).  
   - Internet usage provides an additional boost, especially noticeable in the "Low" marketing category (success rate higher with Internet "Yes").  
   - **Recommendation**: Promote affordable digital marketing training and internet access programs, particularly for low-marketing UMKM.

3. **Encourage Professional Consultation**  
   - Higher Professional Advice frequency (scores 5‚Äì7) shows fluctuating but generally positive correlation with success rate (around 10‚Äì20%) and financial score.  
   - **Recommendation**: Facilitate regular access to mentors, consultants, or business incubators to increase consultation frequency.
